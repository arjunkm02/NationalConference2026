How to Register

Steps to Register

Step 01

Visit the Official Website

Go to our official conference portal at
nationalconferencemca2026.vercel.app

Step 02

Begin Registration

Click on "Register Here" on the homepage to open the registration portal.

Step 03

Fill the Registration Form

Accurately fill all mandatory fields — name, institution, contact details, and paper information.

Step 04

Upload Your Paper

Attach your research paper in the required format as specified in the submission guidelines.

Step 05

Complete Payment

Make the registration payment of Rs. 300/- using the QR code provided on the form.

Step 06

Submit Your Entry

Review all details, then click "Submit" to finalise your registration.

Step 07

Await Confirmation

If your paper is accepted, you will receive a notification via email with further details.

Ensure all submitted details are accurate before clicking Submit. For any queries, please reach out to the conference coordinators via the Contact page.
