About the Conference

Where Ideas Converge,
Innovation Emerges

The National Conference on Recent Trends in Intelligent Computing and Cyber Security (NCRTICCS-2026) is a distinguished platform that brings together Experts, Researchers, Industry Leaders and Students from various states in India to engage in a comprehensive exchange of ideas and knowledge.

With a focus on advancing the field of Intelligent Computing and Cyber Security, the conference provides an opportunity for showcasing cutting-edge research, innovative solutions, and impactful practices. NCRTICCS-2026 warmly encourages Students, Research Fellows and Academicians to participate and contribute.

Key Objectives

01

To provide a forum for researchers, academicians and practitioners to present their latest research findings and innovations in intelligent computing and cyber security.

02

To foster interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange among experts from academia and industry.

03

To discuss emerging trends, challenges and opportunities in intelligent computing and cybersecurity, and identify potential solutions and strategies.

04

To promote awareness and understanding of ethical, legal and societal implications associated with the deployment of intelligent computing technologies.
