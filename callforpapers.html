We invite original, unpublished research contributions in Intelligent Computing and Cyber Security. Share your findings with a national community of researchers and practitioners.

Scope & Topics

Areas of Interest

Authors are solicited to contribute by submitting articles that illustrate research results, project experiments, and industrial experiences in the following areas — but not limited to.

Intelligent Computing & Security
  • Machine learning for intrusion prevention/detection
  • Pattern recognition and anomaly detection
  • AI and cloud computing security
  • Explainable AI
  • Game theory for cybersecurity
Cyber Security & Analytics
  • Malware analysis using ML
  • NLP in Cyber Security
  • Threat monitoring and analysis
  • Trusted networking security and privacy
  • Intelligent agents & multi-agent systems
Educational & Workforce Development
  • Cyber Security Education and Training
  • Skill Development and Capacity Building
  • Diversity and Inclusion in Cyber Security
  • Career Opportunities in AI and Cyber Security
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration
Others
  • Topics to be announced. Stay tuned.

Submission Guidelines

Submit full-length, original, unpublished research articles in English for presentation at the conference.

Papers must follow the IEEE format — minimum 4 pages, maximum 10 pages, including figures, tables and references.

Submit in MS Word format (.docx) as per the prescribed template.

Both experimental and theoretical research contributions are welcome.
